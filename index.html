Notes: Gini coefficient measures post-tax income inequality (0 = perfect equality, 100 = perfect inequality).   Poverty rate = share of population below 50% of national median income (OECD standard).   Maternal mortality per 100,000 live births (WHO/World Bank, 2022–23).   PISA 2022: average of math, reading & science scores (OECD average ≈ 484; a 30-point gap ≈ ~1 year of schooling).   Tertiary attainment = % of 25–64-year-olds with any post-secondary degree (OECD 2024).   HS Graduation = % of adults 25–64 who completed upper secondary education.   Tuition/Student Debt column describes the dominant model; per-borrower debt varies widely by program. US figure (~$38K) is average federal debt for bachelor's graduates.   All figures are approximations and may vary slightly by source and year.   The term "socialist hybrid" is contested — these countries are capitalist market economies with expansive welfare states.
